HCID.

The Centre for Human-Computer Interaction Design

The Centre for HCI Design is an elite Human-Computer Interaction group in London. We take great pride in our outstanding research, teaching, and consultancy & business services. Our prime focus is the relationship between people and innovative technology with the aim of creating more useful and usable systems.

With a mixture of research and practical work, we foster our tight-knit culture through an overarching philosophy. This recognises what we do as a collective and ongoing endeavour, spurred on by the possibilities for enlarging human capacity and creating the conditions for better ways of living together.
