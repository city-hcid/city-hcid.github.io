There are numerous sources to investigate for funding opportunities. The following should be seen as a starting point.

  • Digital Economy (EPSRC)
  • Fund for International Collaboration
  • Future Leaders Fellowships
  • Global Challenges Research Fund (GCRF)

Funding bodies:

  • Engineering and Physical Sciences Research Council (EPSRC)
  • UK Research and Innovation (UKRI - see funding finder)
  • European Research Council (ERC)
  • Leverhulme Trust
  • Wellcome Trust
