PhD Programme

We encourage and supervise a diversity of PhD research related to designing for a better future, innovative interactive technologies, creative methods, co-design, and the study of sociotechnical domains. We pride ourselves on creating a supportive culture for postgraduate research that is committed to both individual and collective development.

HCID staff members are happy to correspond, talk about their research interests, and offer advice on how to apply to study for a PhD. For further information about potential PhD supervisors, see our list of academic staff here.

Applying

For information and to apply to the HCID PhD programme, please visit the Computer Science departmental pages.
