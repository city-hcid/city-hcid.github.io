Results

The results of the REF, published in December 2021, will be broken down as follows:*

  1. Quality profile: Show the proportions of research activity judged to meet each of four starred quality levels.
  2. Sub-profiles: Show the proportions of research activity judged to meet each of four starred quality levels under the threeassessment criteria (outputs, impact and environment).
  3. FTE number: Staff included in the submission.
  4. Category A submitted staff: Proportion of the eligible staff included as Category A submitted staff in the submission.

In addition, in early 2021, concise feedback on each submission will be provided.
